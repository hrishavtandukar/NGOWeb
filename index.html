Trekking

Annapurna & Dhaulagiri Trekking

Majestic Dawn views of Annapurna, South Macchaphuchre and great view of dhaulagiri from base to summit as it stands with pride in the deeepest gorge in the world.

Annapurna Base Camp Trekking

To visit the stronghold of Annapurna Base Camp also popularly recognized as ABC trek through the rice fields, villages while viewing a high peak panaroma.

Annapurna Panaroma(Poon hill) Trekking

The famous hill which stands at 3,210m to reach this destination you must walk through one of the Nepal's biggest Rhododendron's forest while enjoying amazing views of himalayas.

Annapurna Circuit Trekking

Climbing through one of Nepal's highest trekking pass Thorung Las Pass(5,416m) and with the spectactular view of 7000m+ peaks of complete Annapurna massif itself.

Everest Base Camp & Gokyo Lakes Trekking

Trek to the heart of the Everest reigon and enjoy the thrilling and beautiful view of sacred glacial lakes with the 360 degree view from Gokyo Ri.

Everest Base Camp Trekking

Follow the famous luring trail to Everest Base Camp by the beautiful landscapes and climb to the summit of the kalapathar for an unforgettable view of some of worlds highest mountains

Langtang Valley Trekking

The trekkers traverses from west to east crossing extremely diverse landscapes rice terraces, rhodendron forests and in highest reigon you will find a rock with an icredible backdrop of snowy peaks.

Tamang Heritage Trail Trekking

Langtang National park has recently been developed into the Tamang Heritage trail the people in this area are the mostly of tamang and tibetan origin whose culture goes way back many hundreds of years.
